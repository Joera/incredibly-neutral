Meetups are foremost about the ineraction with other people, meeting each other, learning from each other. Each meetup will have a topic, a central question or dillemma. We start with a central introduction and then break out in smaller groups to work on a case study. At the end we come together and present our ideas and findings.

We do not expect you to come up with ground breaking solutions or brilliant start up ideas. It is foremost about listening to other perspectives and learning to better articulate your own. In time we can learn how to formulate our thoughts as a community and grow into something of a decentralized research group or think tank.

Some of us have been discussing these topics for some time. Others are new. Professionals from other sectors who want to learn more are most welcome. So are power users, as well as criticastors and sceptics. The train is running though and won't stop so be prepared to jump on. These events are not for onboarding, speculative 'alpha' or shilling.
